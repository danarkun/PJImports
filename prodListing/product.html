2 MW Spätburgunder (Pinot Noir) 2015 12 months maturation in small used French oak barrels enhances the structure of the wine without influencing the superb ripe cherry fruit. Very fine tannins make it smooth and silky, thanks to the extended lees contact it shows roundness and creaminess. The wine displays elegance and finesse with a Long and lingering finish – it is fruit- driven with charm.

Drink 2017 - 2022

Matches perfectly with poultry, beef and pork also with soft cheese

Alc: 13.21% - RS: 3.7 g/L - Acidity 5.0 g/L

Start with a careful selection of the vineyards: Loose soils give the wines richness and power, whereas calcarious soils give the wines elegance and complexity. Let the fermentation start on its own,. A slow fermentation and a long contact with the lees provide richness and longevity. This Grauburgunder shows its class not only on the palate, but also on the nose: Intense aromas of stone fruit and yellow apple, some tropical fruit (mango) together with earthy notes. Mouth filling on the palate with a generous fruit, terroir notes and elegant herbal spicyness. Creamy and round with great structure, deep and complex, long and lingering multi-layered finish.

Drink 2017 - 2021.

Goes well with white meats, potatoes, asparagus, cheese and grilled fish.

Alc: 13,15% RS: 6.7 g/L Acidity: 5,5 g/L
